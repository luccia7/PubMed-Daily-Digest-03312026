Systematic review and meta-analysis 👥 25 studies (23 RCTs, 2 observational)
Safety of GLP-1 receptor agonists in type 1 diabetes: a systematic review and meta-analysis.
Ramya Kateel, Amrita Parida, Bharti Chogtu, et al. — Diabetes research and clinical practice May 2026
🏥 PopulationPatients with Type 1 Diabetes Mellitus
💊 MedicationsGLP-1 receptor agonists
🌍 Country / DatasetMultiple (systematic review through June 2025)
🔬 Key FindingsSystematic review and meta-analysis of 25 studies including 23 RCTs and 2 observational studies evaluated safety of GLP-1 RAs in T1DM. Updated pooled data established a neutral risk profile regarding hypoglycemia and diabetic ketoacidosis, addressing key safety concerns limiting their adoption as adjunct therapy in Type 1 Diabetes.
Retrospective cohort study 👥 Not reported
Pre-Pregnancy GLP-1 Receptor Agonist or Tirzepatide Use and Gestational Diabetes Risk: Evaluating Pharmacodynamic Carry-Over Versus Post-Discontinuation Metabolic Rebound in a Multinational Federated Cohort.
Mainak Banerjee, Sulagna Dutta, Sujoy Dasgupta — Diabetes, obesity & metabolism May 2026
🏥 PopulationWomen aged 18-45 years without pre-existing diabetes evaluating preconception GLP-1RA/tirzepatide use
💊 MedicationsGLP-1 receptor agonists, tirzepatide
🌍 Country / DatasetTriNetX multinational federated cohort (January 1, 2015-April 30, 2025)
🔬 Key FindingsRetrospective cohort study evaluated association between preconception GLP-1RA/tirzepatide use and gestational diabetes risk using propensity score matching. Study examined pharmacodynamic carry-over effects versus post-discontinuation metabolic rebound in women with 6-18 months preconception medication use and pre-pregnancy discontinuation.
Real-world study 👥 Not reported (claims database study)
Adherence and Persistence Among Individuals Living With Type 2 Diabetes and Using a Free Combination of Basal Insulin and an Injectable Glucagon-Like Peptide-1 Receptor Agonist Versus a Fixed-Ratio Combination Therapy: A Japanese Database Study.
Takeshi Miyatsuka, James Brash, Eleanor Davies, et al. — Diabetes, obesity & metabolism May 2026
🏥 PopulationAdults aged ≥18 years with type 2 diabetes using basal insulin and GLP-1 receptor agonist therapy in Japan
💊 MedicationsFree combination therapy (basal insulin plus injectable GLP-1 RA) versus fixed-ratio combination therapy (basal insulin plus GLP-1 RA)
🌍 Country / DatasetJapan (DeSC-IQVIA Integrated claims data, October 2019 to September 2023)
🔬 Key FindingsRetrospective observational cohort study compared adherence and persistence between free combination therapy (daily basal insulin plus injectable GLP-1 RA) versus fixed-ratio combination therapy in Japanese patients with type 2 diabetes, examining differences in treatment continuation and medication adherence patterns.
Retrospective cohort study 👥 Not reported
Glucagon-Like Peptide-1 Receptor Agonists and Outcomes After Intracerebral Hemorrhage in Patients With Type 2 Diabetes: A Propensity-Matched Cohort Study.
Fnu Ruchika, David Lee, James Feghali, et al. — Neurosurgery May 2026
🏥 PopulationPatients with intracerebral hemorrhage and type 2 diabetes mellitus
💊 MedicationsGLP-1 receptor agonists versus other hypoglycemic drugs including insulin
🌍 Country / DatasetTriNetX global network database
🔬 Key FindingsRetrospective propensity-matched cohort study compared post-intracerebral hemorrhage outcomes in patients with T2DM receiving GLP-1RAs versus other hypoglycemic drugs, examining GLP-1RA exposure within 5 years before ICH onset and assessing neuroprotective and anti-inflammatory effects on patient outcomes.
